Our Tradition

At InkaBrasa, our story traces back to a passion for ancestral recipes and authentic Peruvian cuisine. Founded over a decade ago along the iconic Costa Verde, our deluxe rotisserie was born from the dream of preserving and sharing the unparalleled flavors of our land. From day one, we have been dedicated to offering rotisserie chicken that combines traditional techniques with fresh, high-quality ingredients to create a culinary experience that celebrates our roots. Our tradition is more than a recipe; it is a tribute to Peruvian heritage and a commitment to excellence. With every bite, we invite our diners on a gastronomic journey that evokes the warmth, flavor, and spirit of Peru.
